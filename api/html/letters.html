How it works
Three simple steps for each sign.
1. Choose a letter – This tells the model which sign you’re trying to make.
2. Hold your sign – On the webcam page, press “Start capture” and hold your hand sign steady until the progress bar finishes.
3. Read the feedback – The app compares your sign with the selected letter and lets you know if it matches.
Tips for best results:
  • Make sure your hand is fully inside the camera frame.
  • Hold the sign steady until capture completes.
  • Use good lighting so your hand is clearly visible.
