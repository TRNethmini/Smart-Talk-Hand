Getting Started
Three simple steps to predict any classification sign.
1. Choose a model – Switch between dynamic ML datasets (e.g., SSL Colors).
2. Select a sign – Click what sign class you want to practice.
3. Read the feedback – Hold your sign firmly in the camera frame to receive instant algorithmic validation.
Tips for highest accuracy:
  • If the dataset is two hands, ensure both hands are consistently visible.
  • Stand centered beneath adequate natural lighting.
  • Do not obscure the camera.
